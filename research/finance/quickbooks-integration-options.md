# QuickBooks Integration Options for Labor Timekeeper

**Research Date:** February 2026  
**Prepared for:** JCW Construction

---

## Executive Summary

QuickBooks offers multiple integration pathways for construction companies to connect their time tracking systems with accounting and invoicing. The most suitable options for JCW Construction range from native QuickBooks Construction Edition features to third-party middleware solutions. Integration enables automated invoicing, synchronized cost data, and streamlined payroll processing, reducing manual data entry and improving financial accuracy.

---

## Key Findings

### 1. QuickBooks Construction Edition (Recommended)

QuickBooks has a dedicated Construction Edition that includes:
- **Job Costing Module**: Built-in tracking for labor, materials, and subcontractor costs
- **Progress Invoicing**: Create invoices based on project completion percentage
- **Time Tracking Integration**: Connect with industry-specific time tracking apps
- **Cost Codes**: Pre-configured CSI MasterFormat coding system
- **Change Order Management**: Track scope changes and cost impacts

**Pricing**: Starts at $100/month for the Contractor tier

### 2. Integration Methods

#### Direct Integration (API)
- Use QuickBooks Online API to push time entries directly
- Requires developer knowledge or third-party connector
- Most flexible but highest implementation cost

#### Middleware Solutions
- **Syncari**: Cloud-based data orchestration platform
- **Tray.io**: Workflow automation with QuickBooks connector
- **Workato**: Enterprise integration platform
- **Celigo**: QuickBooks integrator with pre-built templates

#### Native Export/Import
- Export Labor Timekeeper data to Excel/CSV
- Import into QuickBooks manually
- Lowest cost but most manual effort

### 3. Data Flow Options

**Labor to QuickBooks**:
- Hours worked by job and cost code
- Employee wage rates and benefits
- Labor burden rates (taxes, insurance, benefits)

**QuickBooks to Labor**:
- Customer and project information
- Budgeted costs and milestones
- Subcontractor rates

### 4. Construction-Specific Features

- **Phase/Job Tracking**: Track costs by project phase
- **Class Tracking**: Categorize by division, location, or crew
- **Item Tracking**: Link inventory items to projects
- **Purchase Orders**: Track materials to specific jobs

---

## Recommendations for JCW Construction

1. **Evaluate QuickBooks Construction Edition**: Consider upgrading to the Contractor tier for built-in job costing
2. **Start with CSV Export**: Begin with manual export/import, then automate as volume increases
3. **Map Cost Codes**: Ensure Labor Timekeeper cost codes align with QuickBooks items
4. **Implement Progress Invoicing**: Use percentage-of-completion for large projects
5. **Automate Weekly**: Set up weekly data synchronization process
6. **Test Integration**: Run parallel manual and automated processes for 30 days
