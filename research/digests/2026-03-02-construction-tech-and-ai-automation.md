2. **Human-AI Collaboration Models**: Shift from full automation to augmented intelligence approaches, maintaining human oversight for complex decisions
3. **Edge Computing Adoption**: 73% of new construction tech deployments utilizing edge processing to handle latency-sensitive applications

- **Investment Surge**: $4.7B invested in construction tech startups in 2024 (65% increase YoY)

- **Standardization**: Lack of common protocols between equipment manufacturers limiting interoperability

- **Safety Validation**: Limited long-term safety data for autonomous equipment in mixed human-robot environments
