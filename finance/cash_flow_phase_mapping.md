# Cash Flow Phase Mapping

## Preconstruction
- **Inflows**: owner deposit/retainer
- **Outflows**: design, survey, permits, insurance

## Foundation
- **Inflows**: foundation milestone draw
- **Outflows**: concrete, rebar, excavation, subs

## Framing
- **Inflows**: progress draw(s)
- **Outflows**: lumber, labor, trusses

## Windows/Enclosure
- **Inflows**: dried-in milestone draw
- **Outflows**: windows, doors, envelope materials

## MEP
- **Inflows**: rough-in draw(s)
- **Outflows**: trade subs, equipment, inspections

## Drywall
- **Inflows**: drywall start/completion draw
- **Outflows**: labor, material, waste

## Finishes
- **Inflows**: partial draws + final payment
- **Outflows**: finishes, fixtures, punchlist
