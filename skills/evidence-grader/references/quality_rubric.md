# Evidence Quality Rubric

## Reliability Score
- 5: Primary source with methods or data, official docs, or author talk.
- 4: Reputable secondary source with clear citations to primary sources.
- 3: Secondary source with limited citations or unclear methods.
- 2: Commentary or summary without evidence.
- 1: Anecdotal or unverifiable claim.

## Relevance Score
- 5: Directly answers the research question.
- 4: Strongly related with minor gaps.
- 3: Related but requires inference.
- 2: Tangentially related.
- 1: Not relevant.

## Recommendation Rules
- Use: Reliability 4-5 and Relevance 4-5.
- Verify: Reliability 3-4 or Relevance 3-4.
- Avoid: Reliability 1-2 or Relevance 1-2.
