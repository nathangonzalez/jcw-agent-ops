2. **Edge Computing Dominance**: Real-time processing requirements driving on-site computing solutions
3. **Safety-First Automation**: Regulatory compliance driving AI adoption in safety monitoring applications

### Technical Barriers Identified

2. **Interoperability**: Legacy systems integration remains major hurdle
3. **Environmental Robustness**: Harsh construction conditions challenging for consumer-grade AI hardware
