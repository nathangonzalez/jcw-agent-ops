## Proposed Spikes

### Spike 1: Multi-Robot Coordination Framework
**Objective**: Develop coordination algorithms for multiple autonomous construction robots operating simultaneously
**Rationale**: Current solutions focus on single-robot applications; job sites require coordinated multi-robot workflows
**Success Criteria**: 
- Collision-free path planning for ≥3 robots
- 90% task completion efficiency vs. sequential operation
- Real-time task reallocation capabilities
**Estimated Effort**: 8-10 weeks
**Risk Level**: Medium-High (coordination complexity, safety considerations)

### Spike 2: Predictive Material Logistics Using ML
**Objective**: Create ML model for just-in-time material delivery prediction
**Rationale**: Material delays account for 23% of project overruns; weather, traffic, and demand patterns are predictable
**Success Criteria**:
- 85% accuracy in delivery time predictions
- 15% reduction in on-site storage requirements
- Integration with existing ERP systems
**Estimated Effort**: 6-8 weeks
**Risk Level**: Medium (data quality dependencies, supplier integration)

### Spike 3: AR-Assisted Quality Control System
**Objective**: Develop AR interface for real-time construction quality verification
**Rationale**: Quality issues detected late in process cost 10x more to fix; AR can provide instant BIM comparison
**Success Criteria**:
- Real-time deviation detection with ±5mm accuracy
- Intuitive AR interface for field workers
- Automated documentation generation
**Estimated Effort**: 10-12 weeks
**Risk Level**: Medium (AR hardware limitations, lighting conditions)

### Spike 4: Federated Learning for Construction Safety
**Objective**: Implement privacy-preserving ML model training across multiple construction sites
**Rationale**: Safety models improve with more data, but companies reluctant to share proprietary information
**Success Criteria**:
- Maintain data privacy while improving model accuracy
- 20% improvement in incident prediction vs. single-site models
- Demonstrate with 3+ construction partners
**Estimated Effort**: 12-14 weeks
**Risk Level**: High (privacy regulations, partner coordination, novel technology)
