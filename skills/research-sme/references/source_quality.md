# Source Quality Rubric

Use this rubric to rate source reliability and relevance.

## Reliability Tiers
- Tier A: Primary sources, official docs, original papers, author talks, or direct datasets.
- Tier B: Reputable secondary analysis that cites primary sources.
- Tier C: Tertiary summaries or commentary without primary evidence.

## Recency
- Prefer items from the last 12 months unless a classic reference is required.

## Evidence Checks
- Confirm author identity and affiliation when possible.
- Prefer sources with reproducible methods or data.
- Cross-check important claims across at least two sources.

## Use Rules
- Use Tier A whenever possible.
- Use Tier B only if Tier A is unavailable.
- Avoid Tier C unless explicitly requested.
