## Background & Context

### Market Landscape
The construction industry loses approximately $177 billion annually due to poor project management and inefficient time tracking, according to McKinsey Global Institute research. Traditional time tracking methods—paper timesheets, badge systems, and mobile apps—face significant challenges in construction environments:

- **Environmental barriers**: Dust, moisture, extreme temperatures
- **Safety concerns**: Hands-free operation requirements
- **Workforce demographics**: 73% of construction workers prefer voice interaction over touchscreen interfaces (Construction Dive, 2023)
- **Mobility constraints**: Workers frequently move between locations and elevation levels

### Technology Evolution
Voice-first interfaces have matured significantly since 2020, driven by advances in:
