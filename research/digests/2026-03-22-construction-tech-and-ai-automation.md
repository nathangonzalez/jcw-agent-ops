## Key Insights

### 1. **Convergence of Technologies Driving Adoption**
Multiple sources indicate that the integration of AI, IoT, and robotics is reaching a tipping point. The combination of computer vision + BIM + IoT sensors is achieving practical accuracy thresholds (>90%) that make business cases compelling.

### 2. **Predictive Maintenance Emerging as Primary ROI Driver**
Evidence from multiple papers shows predictive maintenance delivering measurable ROI (18-31% cost reduction) faster than other AI applications, making it the leading adoption pathway for contractors.

### 3. **Safety Applications Show Highest Accuracy Rates**
PPE compliance monitoring and hazard detection systems consistently demonstrate >95% accuracy rates, suggesting mature technology readiness for widespread deployment.

### 4. **Skills Gap Creating Implementation Barriers**
Industry sources consistently cite lack of technical expertise as primary barrier to adoption, with 67% of contractors reporting insufficient AI/automation knowledge.
