1. **Convergence Trend**: Digital twins + AI showing strongest ROI (15-25% project cost reduction)

2. **Safety Applications Leading Adoption**: Computer vision for safety monitoring has highest implementation rate
   - 47% of surveyed firms deploying vision-based safety systems

3. **Skills Gap Widening**: Technical talent shortage limiting AI adoption

   - Average salary premium: 34% above traditional roles

4. **Regulatory Lag**: Current building codes not addressing AI-automated construction
   - Only 3 US states have specific provisions for robotic construction
   - EU leading with comprehensive framework expected 2025
