# Ledger Taxonomy v1

## Preconstruction
- Design & engineering fees
- Survey and site studies
- Permit & entitlement costs
- Insurance & bonding setup
- Bid/estimating costs

## Foundation
- Sitework, grading, earthwork
- Concrete & rebar purchases
- Foundation subcontractor payments
- Equipment rental (foundation)

## Framing
- Structural materials (lumber, steel)
- Framing subcontractors
- Truss and hardware purchases
- Equipment rental (framing)

## Windows/Enclosure
- Window/door purchases
- Envelope/waterproofing materials
- Glazing subcontractors
- Exterior door hardware

## MEP
- MEP subcontractors (labor)
- Equipment/materials (HVAC, rough electrical/plumbing)
- Controls, fire protection, inspections

## Drywall
- Drywall materials
- Drywall subcontractors
- Finishing (tape, mud, texture)
- Waste disposal (drywall phase)

## Finishes
- Paint, flooring, ceiling finishes
- Millwork, cabinetry, doors/hardware
- Final plumbing & lighting fixtures
- Appliances, specialty equipment
- Final clean & punchlist
